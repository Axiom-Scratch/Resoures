You don’t need to read the whole OpenGL manual 😄  
For GP math, just learn the basics: **vectors, matrices, dot/cross, trig**, and **transformations** (translate/rotate/scale).

## Good math resources
- **3Blue1Brown (Essence of Linear Algebra):** https://www.3blue1brown.com/topics/linear-algebra  
- **Khan Academy (Linear Algebra):** https://www.khanacademy.org/math/linear-algebra  
- **Immersive Linear Algebra (super visual):** https://immersivemath.com/ila/  
- **LearnOpenGL (Transformations):** https://learnopengl.com/Getting-started/Transformations  
- **Freya Holmér (Vector math for game dev):** https://www.youtube.com/@Acegikmo  

Start with **vec2/vec3**, then **mat4**, then **camera** later.
